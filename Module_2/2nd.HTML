Strawberry Shortcake: A Timeless Treat

Strawberry Shortcake is a beloved dessert made with layers of tender cake, juicy fresh strawberries, and lightly sweetened whipped cream. This recipe serves 8 and is perfect for summer gatherings or a simple family treat.

The secret to a perfect strawberry shortcake is using ripe strawberries and freshly whipped cream.
- Home Baking Expert

Test Kitchen Tips

  • Use cold butter for a tender and flaky shortcake texture.
  • Let strawberries macerate for at least 15 minutes for extra sweetness and juice.
  • Whip cream just until stiff peaks form to avoid overbeating.
  • For best results, assemble just before serving to keep the cake from getting soggy.
