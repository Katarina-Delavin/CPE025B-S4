The Little Red Hen

Listen and Read

Use the player below to listen to the story while reading the text.

Part 1: The Hen's Discovery

Once upon a time, there was a little red hen who lived on a farm. One day, she found some grains of wheat.

"Who will help me plant this wheat?" asked the hen.

"Not I," said the dog. "Not I," said the cat. "Not I," said the duck.

Part 2: Planting and Growing

So the little red hen planted the wheat all by herself. She watered it and watched it grow into tall stalks.

When the wheat was ready, she asked, "Who will help me harvest the wheat?"

"Not I," said the dog. "Not I," said the cat. "Not I," said the duck.

Part 3: Making Bread

The hen took the wheat to the mill and ground it into flour. Then she made dough and baked it into bread.

When the bread was done, she asked, "Who will help me eat this bread?"

"I will!" said the dog. "I will!" said the cat. "I will!" said the duck.

But the little red hen said, "No, you didn't help me plant or harvest or bake. You can watch me eat it all by myself."

And she did. The end.
